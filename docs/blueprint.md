# **App Name**: MASI Insights

## Core Features:

- Bourse Article Feed: Curate articles focused on Moroccan stock market analysis, sector insights, and MASI index trends.
- Bank Comparator: Compare Moroccan banks based on fees and accessibility for checking accounts.
- Brokerage Comparator: Compare Moroccan brokerages, listing fees, commissions, platform features and responsiveness.
- Investment Course Promotion: List available courses that teach Moroccans to invest, such as 'how to invest your first salary'.
- Fee simulator Tool: Generative AI tool for determining the impact of a bank's fees on total returns.
- Investment News Summarizer: AI-powered summarization of Moroccan market news for newsletter content, using external sources as tools.
- Weekly Newsletter: Deliver weekly market insights, promotional offers for courses/coaching, Black Friday announcements, and new service updates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated olive green (#A6B31E), referencing growth and the Moroccan landscape.
- Background color: Very light, desaturated olive (#F2F4E5), creating a neutral canvas.
- Accent color: Desaturated gold (#C9B037), evoking Moroccan artistry and the history of the Dirham.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif for a modern, avant-garde look.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, chosen to promote readability and impart a bit of warmth or personality.
- Use clear, professional icons to represent financial concepts, ensuring easy recognition.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout with ample white space for readability and ease of navigation.
